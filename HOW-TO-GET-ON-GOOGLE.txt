============================================================
STEP 2: HOST THE WEBSITE (make it live on the internet)
============================================================

Your site is static HTML so you can host it FREE:

OPTION A — GitHub Pages (free, easiest since your code is already on GitHub)
  1. Go to github.com/mchadz1z9/waterworks-site
  2. Click "Settings" tab at the top
  3. Click "Pages" in the left sidebar
  4. Under "Source" select "Deploy from a branch"
  5. Select "master" branch, folder "/ (root)"
  6. Click Save
  7. Your site will be live at: mchadz1z9.github.io/waterworks-site
  8. To use your custom domain: on the same Pages settings page,
     type your domain in the "Custom domain" field
  9. Add a CNAME record at your domain registrar pointing to
     mchadz1z9.github.io

OPTION B — Netlify (free, drag and drop)
  1. Go to netlify.com and sign up (use your GitHub account)
  2. Click "Add new site" > "Import an existing project"
  3. Connect your GitHub repo mchadz1z9/waterworks-site
  4. Click Deploy — done, your site is live
  5. To use your custom domain: go to Site Settings > Domain Management
  6. Add your domain and follow the DNS instructions


============================================================
STEP 3: GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE (this is how Google finds you)
============================================================

This is the most important step. Without this, Google doesn't
know your site exists.

  1. Go to: search.google.com/search-console
  2. Sign in with a Google account
  3. Click "Add property"
  4. Choose "URL prefix" and enter your full URL
     (e.g. https://waterworksandlawncare.com)
  5. Verify ownership — easiest method:
     - Download the HTML verification file Google gives you
     - Put it in your waterworks-site folder
     - Push to GitHub so it goes live
  6. Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
  7. Enter: sitemap.xml
  8. Click Submit
  9. Google will start crawling your pages within a few days

Check back in a week — the "Performance" tab will show what
searches are finding your site and how many clicks you get.


============================================================
STEP 4: GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE (this is how you show on Maps)
============================================================

This puts you in the "local pack" — the map with 3 businesses
that shows at the top when someone searches "lawn care near me"
or "lawn care Thornhill."

  1. Go to: business.google.com
  2. Click "Manage now"
  3. Enter business name: WaterWorks & Lawn Care
  4. Choose category: "Lawn Care Service"
     (you can add more: Pressure Washing Service, Car Wash, etc.)
  5. Service area: Thornhill, Ontario (and surrounding areas)
  6. Since you don't have a storefront, choose "I deliver goods
     and services to my customers" (service area business)
  7. Add your contact email: aidenthebuilder123@gmail.com
  8. Add your website URL
  9. Google will verify you (usually by postcard to your address,
     takes about 1-2 weeks)
  10. Once verified, add:
      - Photos of your work (before/after lawn cuts, pressure
        washing results, clean cars, etc.)
      - Business hours
      - A description with keywords:
        "WaterWorks & Lawn Care provides affordable lawn mowing,
        pressure washing, hand car washes, and snow removal in
        Thornhill, Ontario. Services start at just $10."
      - Ask happy customers to leave Google reviews — this is
        the #1 factor for ranking in local search


============================================================
STEP 5: GET LISTED ON FREE DIRECTORIES
============================================================

These give you "backlinks" which boost your Google ranking:

  - Yelp (yelp.ca) — create a free business listing
  - Yellow Pages (yellowpages.ca) — free listing
  - Homestars (homestars.com) — popular in Ontario for home services
  - Bark (bark.com) — free for service providers
  - Facebook — create a Facebook Business Page
  - Instagram — create a business profile, post before/after photos
  - Kijiji (kijiji.ca) — post your services in the Thornhill area
  - Nextdoor (nextdoor.ca) — neighbourhood app, great for local services

For each listing make sure you use:
  - EXACT same business name: WaterWorks & Lawn Care
  - EXACT same email/phone on every site
  - Your website URL
  - "Thornhill" in the description

Consistency across all listings is important — Google uses this
to verify you are a real business.


============================================================
STEP 6: CONTENT THAT RANKS (optional but powerful)
============================================================

Google ranks sites higher when they have useful content.
Consider adding a simple blog or tips page with posts like:

  - "5 Lawn Care Tips for Thornhill Homeowners"
  - "How Often Should You Pressure Wash Your Driveway?"
  - "Winter Prep: When to Book Snow Removal in Thornhill"
  - "Why Hand Car Washes Are Better Than Drive-Throughs"

Each post targets keywords people actually search for.
Even 3-4 short articles (300-500 words each) can make
a big difference.


============================================================
STEP 7: GET REVIEWS (this is the #1 ranking factor)
============================================================

Google reviews are the single biggest thing that determines
whether you show up in local search results.

After every job:
  1. Text or email the customer a direct link to leave a review
  2. The link format is:
     https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID
     (you get your Place ID from Google Business Profile)
  3. Even 5-10 five-star reviews will put you ahead of most
     competitors who have zero

What to say when asking:
  "Hey! Thanks for choosing WaterWorks. If you were happy with
  the service, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review?
  It really helps us out! Here's the link: [link]"
